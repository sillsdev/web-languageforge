Activity

In Language Forge, Activity is one of the views that people may see if their role permits it. This view shows a list of changes in rows. Each row might be a change made to lexical data, or they may be member role changes that were made in the language projects you own. It includes information about how long ago the change happened, and which dictionary was changed.

Display this view

  1. In the My Projects view, click   (Activity) which is near to the top of the window.

Things you can do in this view

  • When you are in the Activity view, the might include a number.

Example: . The number indicates .....

  • To change the number of displayed row, you can choose (All Activity) or (Show Only My Activity).

  • If the row shows an entry's headword or a particular project, each is typically a hyperlink. Click a hyperlink to jump to the List view in that project, There, you can find that lexical entry.
