Views

In Language Forge, views are windows of lexical data or windows of controls used for project management. Near the top of the Language Forge window, you see a series of items separated by a forward slash (/). This is referred to the "breadcrumb" trail. For example, you might see this: My Projects / <project name> / List. The last item in the breadcrumb is the current view.

Here are some examples of views:

  • List view - Here is where you see a list of lexical entries.

  • Edit Entry view - If you click an entry in the List view, you open that entry in the Edit Entry view. You see a column of lexical entries in the left pane, and the details of the currently selected entry in the right pane. These details appear in fields. To return to the List view, click (Back to List)

  • Comments view - The left pane shows the lexical entry and fields. The right pane shows a place where you choose the context for your comment and a place to enter your comment. Below them are any existing comments that are associated with the current lexical entry. To return to Edit Entry, click (Return to Edit Entry).
