Delete an entry

  1. In the left pane of the Edit Entry view, click the word you want to delete.

  2. In the right pane, make sure the word you want to delete appears in the right pane, and then click (Delete Entry).

The Delete Word confirmation box appear. It displays the word that will be deleted.

  1. In the confirmation box, if you are sure that you want to delete the word, click Delete Word.
