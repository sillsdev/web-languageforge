Add Comments

Do these steps to add a comment to comment to a lexical entry.

  1. In the view that shows a list of lexical entries, click the entry to which you want to add a comment.

  2. Click (Show Comments) to display the Comments view.

The left pane shows the lexical entry, and fields. The right pane shows a place where you choose the context for your comment and a place to enter your comment. Below them are any existing comments that are associated with the current lexical entry.

  1. Do these steps:

    • Click a field in the left pane.

    • Click the text box in the right pane, and then type your comment. Click (Post) to save your comment.

In the list of lexical entries, an orange "speech bubble" icon () is added to the entry.
