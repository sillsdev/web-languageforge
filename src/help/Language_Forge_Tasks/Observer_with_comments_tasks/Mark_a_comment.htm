Mark a comment

Do these steps to mark a comment as resolved or as a comment that has a task that needs to be done.

  1. In the Edit Entry view, click the entry with the comment () you will mark.

  2. Click (Show Comments) to display the Comments view.

  1. In the right pane, move your mouse pointer over the comment you want to mark, and then click one of these buttons:

    • to mark the comment as resolved.

    • to mark the comment as having a task that the needs to be done.
