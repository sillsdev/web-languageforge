Search for entry

Do these steps in the List or Edit view. Each of these views has a Search Entries box where you can type.

  1. Click the Search Entries box, and then do these steps:

    • To clear any contents from the Search Entries box, click the x.

    • Start typing the headword or words that are in the Meaning or Gloss fields of the entry you want to find.

As you type entries that have words that match what you are typing appear in a list. The bottom of the list shows how many matches were found.

  1. When you see the entry you want to find appear in the list, click it.

The entry you clicked opens in the right pane of the Edit view.
